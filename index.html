Pop by the Polls

This is a short research article for the 2020 election. It covers four issues currently being discussed, a candidate running for President of the United States, a polling place, and a sample ballot.

Read More

Issues

These are some of the issues that voters and candidates are discussing this election season.

Climate Change

Climate change is the shifting of global weather patterns driven by human action, especially greenhouse gas emissions.

Abortion

The Supreme Court case Roe v. Wade legalized abortion, but the topic remains a controversial and highly politicized issue.

Marriage Equality

Same sex marriage was legalized by the Supreme Court in 2015, but the decision may soon be overturned.

Gun Control

The level of restrictions on who can purchase a gun and the process of purchasing a gun is highly debated.

Candidate

This is a person who is running for President of the United States in the 2020 general election.

Joe Biden

Joe Biden is the Democratic candidate for the President of the United States in the 2020 eleciton. He was the Vice President of the United States during the Obama administration. His running mate is Senator Kamala Harris.

Polling Place

This is a location that voters can visit to cast a ballot for the candidates they prefer.

Laurel Park Elementary School

Laurel Park Elementary School is the polling place assigned to my address. Residents in my area who choose to vote in person will cast their ballots here. Polling places are often schools or churches and are assigned based on one's address.

Sample Ballot

This is a document that potential voters can use to practice voting and to preview which candidates are up for election.

Wake County Sample Ballot

Sample ballots allow voters to preview the voting process. This is the sample ballot for Wake County in North Carolina. The ballot outlines which candidates are running for each position and notes their party affiliation as well.
